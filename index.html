About

Blockchain Olympiad Bangladesh (BCOLBD) has been held since March 2020 and will arrange its 3rd edition next year in Feb/March 2022. Hundreds of teams compete for the opportunity to win big prizes and learn from the experts every year. Look out for the event announcement and register when participation is declared open.

BUSINESS CASE COMPETITION
Contestants define their own problem scopes and develop plans to solve it.

TRAINING & WORKSHOP
Contestants may avail of Blockchain training and workshop opportunities arranged by Blockchain Olympiad Bangladesh.

CONFERENCE & HACKATHON
Contestants may build something to impress investors and employers.
