BCOLBD 2021 OFFICIAL RULES FOR TEAMS

  1. Home
  2. Rules

A. ELIGIBILITY

Individual Eligibility

Students only: Any current students or recent graduate may register as a competitor; a recent graduate is someone who was a student until 1 year ago (July 4th, 2019).

No age limits.

Representation: An individual may be attached to an academic institution by being enrolled in it; a recognized institution is an accredited educational institution or an institution that may grant a degree or diploma.

Returning Participation: Previous individuals are eligible to compete.

Team Eligibility

Team Composition: Projects are submitted by teams of one to four eligible individuals; one participant can be part of more than one team; each team shall have a team lead, as the main contact person.

Team Uniqueness: Each team must be unique, i.e., a team may not share more than 50% of the same composition as any other team, rounded to the lower number if the team is an odd number; team leads must be unique, i.e., one person cannot be the team lead of multiple teams.

Team Representation: Each team must nominate one representative academic institution if team members are from different academic institutions.

Returning Participation: Previous teams are eligible to compete.

B. PROJECTS

Project Registration

One Team, One Project: A team can submit only one project.

Registration: The registration will be open on Tuesday 1 December 2020.

The registration & submission deadlines for Teams:

Registration deadline: Thursday 31 December 2020 by 11:59 PM.

Whitepaper, posterboard, pitch deck as PDF deadline: Starting Monday 1 February 2021 and ending Wednesday 10 Feb 2021 by 11:59 PM.

Elevator pitch, 10-min pitch, prototype/demo videos deadline: Starting Thursday 11 February 2021 and ending Monday 22 Feb 2021 by 11:59 PM.

Project Evaluation

Evaluation Stage:Primary evaluation will be based on whitepaper and posterboard. Selected projects will be in the finals.

Grading Criteria: Each project shall be evaluated by the following three parts:

20% Pitch Presentation (10-min limit)

30% Whitepaper (10-page limit)

50% Poster + Q&A Session

Prototype Grading Criteria: Proof of concept for parts of backend and frontend.

Project Prizes

Category: Projects may be categorized as follows:

Documentation Authentication

E-governance

FinTech

Identity/Privacy
